Roma eterna

O'er the vast realms of boundless power,
She reigned supreme, in glory's tower;
The mistress of a world's command,
With victory's laurel in her hand.

But now, how low the pride of Rome,
The echo of her former dome.
A faded scene, a broken throne,
Yet still, her majesty is shown.
